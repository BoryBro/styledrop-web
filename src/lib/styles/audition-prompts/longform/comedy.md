# Comedy

## Base — Social Disaster Core

### INTRO
Transform the uploaded photo into a Korean comedy film still built around a socially disastrous but deeply human moment. The result must feel like a real high-resolution cinematic photograph — awkward, relatable, lively, and authentic, with grounded realism and no cartoon exaggeration.

### POSE_EXPRESSION
- Eye-level medium shot
- Subject caught in an unplanned reaction or badly timed moment
- Eyes open and fully readable
- Expression should feel sincerely embarrassed, startled, or awkward rather than exaggerated for slapstick
- Body language should suggest the character wishes they could immediately undo what just happened

### SCENE
- Everyday Korean environment such as a cafe, restaurant, office corridor, apartment entryway, family dining room, convenience store, or public waiting area
- The setting should be familiar and socially legible so the humor lands instantly
- Production design must remain realistic, modest, and grounded in ordinary life
- The frame should feel like a real moment of public or interpersonal embarrassment rather than a stylized comedy set

### LIGHTING
- Natural indoor practical lighting or realistic daylight-balanced light
- The face must remain highly readable because expression is central to the comedy
- Light should feel ordinary, honest, and situational rather than glamorous
- Avoid dramatic chiaroscuro, theatrical comedy spotlighting, or artificial beauty glow

### FILTER_DREAM_EFFECT
- Remove dreamy or fantasy treatment and replace it with polished live-action comedy realism
- Add only subtle film grain, slight highlight softness, and a light reduction of digital harshness
- Keep the image clear enough for timing, reaction, and social context to read immediately
- Avoid plasticky retouching, ad-like cleanliness, cartoon saturation, or sitcom artificiality

### PHOTO_STYLE
- High-resolution Korean comedy feature-film or premium drama-comedy still
- Moderate depth of field with enough environmental context to explain the awkward situation
- Real photographic rendering only
- The final result should feel observational, spontaneous, and painfully human rather than staged like a poster

### COLOR
- Everyday daylight neutrals
- Soft warm indoor practical tones
- Light urban beiges, muted grays, and modest natural color variation
- Skin should remain natural and readable without glamour grading

### MOOD
- Awkward
- Relatable
- Human
- Socially painful
- Funny
- Lovable in spite of the embarrassment

### STRICT_RULES_APPEND
- Do not cartoonize the face, pose, or surrounding environment
- Do not add random slapstick props or visual gags unrelated to the scene
- Keep the humor rooted in human timing, reaction, and social realism

### OUTPUT_TARGET_APPEND
- Ultra-realistic Korean comedy still centered on an awkward social disaster
- Same recognizable uploaded user preserved inside a believable comic situation
- Realistic timing, expression clarity, and grounded live-action humor should define the final result
