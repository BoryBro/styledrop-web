# Daily

## Base — Everyday Realism Core

### INTRO
Transform the uploaded photo into a Korean slice-of-life still built around ordinary life observed with care. The result must feel like a real high-resolution cinematic photograph — calm, familiar, grounded, and emotionally truthful, with no unnecessary genre exaggeration or decorative stylization.

### POSE_EXPRESSION
- Eye-level medium shot
- Natural everyday posture with no overt posing
- Eyes open and present in the moment
- Expression should feel neutral-to-soft, quietly thoughtful, or naturally attentive rather than dramatically emotional
- Body language should feel lived-in, believable, and unperformed

### SCENE
- Everyday Korean environment such as an apartment corner, neighborhood street, kitchen, bus stop, hallway, rooftop edge, convenience store frontage, or local bench
- Production design should feel modest, real, and lightly lived-in without art-direction excess
- The setting must immediately read as daily life rather than cinematic spectacle
- The frame should find meaning inside the ordinary, not through dramatic visual devices

### LIGHTING
- Soft natural daylight or believable practical interior light
- Gentle face readability without glamor shaping
- Lighting should feel observational, breathable, and honest
- Avoid theatrical contrast, fantasy haze, or beauty-oriented glow

### FILTER_DREAM_EFFECT
- Remove dreamy spectacle and replace it with restrained slice-of-life polish
- Add fine grain, slight textural cohesion, and a mild reduction of harsh digital sharpness
- Preserve ordinary realism in skin, clothing, walls, and environmental surfaces
- Avoid beauty blur, sentimental wash, heavy bloom, or stylized atmospheric tricks

### PHOTO_STYLE
- High-resolution Korean everyday-drama or slice-of-life still
- Moderate depth of field with clear subject readability and enough environmental context
- Real photographic rendering only
- The image should feel expensive through restraint, not visual excess

### COLOR
- Soft daylight blue
- Warm gray
- Ivory
- Muted neighborhood neutrals
- Natural skin tones with low-aggression grading

### MOOD
- Familiar
- Calm
- Observant
- Modest
- Human
- Quietly warm

### STRICT_RULES_APPEND
- Do not glamorize daily life into fashion-editorial imagery
- Do not force sentimentality through haze, glow, or over-romantic grading
- Keep the frame truthful, lived-in, and emotionally restrained

### OUTPUT_TARGET_APPEND
- Ultra-realistic Korean slice-of-life still defined by everyday familiarity
- Same recognizable uploaded user preserved in a believable daily frame
- Realism, restraint, and emotional truth should define the final result
