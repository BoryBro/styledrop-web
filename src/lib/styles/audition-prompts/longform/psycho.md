# Psycho

## Base — Psychological Pressure Core

### INTRO
Transform the uploaded photo into a Korean psychological-thriller still built around internal pressure and unstable self-control. The result must feel like a real high-resolution cinematic photograph — severe, tense, intimate, and psychologically loaded, while remaining fully grounded in live-action realism.

### POSE_EXPRESSION
- Eye-level medium shot
- Subject holding still with visible internal restraint
- Eyes open and mentally active
- Expression should feel controlled on the surface but strained underneath
- Body language should suggest the character is suppressing something unstable rather than openly performing emotion

### SCENE
- Realistic interior such as an apartment room, office, waiting area, corridor, elevator zone, or emotionally cold institutional space
- The setting should feel plausible, normal, and psychologically unfriendly
- Hard surfaces, empty space, reflective planes, or geometric architectural lines may support the tension
- The environment must remain real rather than symbolic or surreal

### LIGHTING
- Directional practical or daylight-motivated light
- Uneven emotional balance across the face is acceptable as long as identity remains fully readable
- Light should emphasize stress, mental friction, and private pressure without using fantasy effects
- Avoid theatrical horror-lighting or symbolic visual gimmicks

### FILTER_DREAM_EFFECT
- Replace dreamy softness with severe psychological realism
- Add fine grain, restrained atmosphere, and slight optical softness only where physically believable
- Preserve real skin texture, fatigue, mouth tension, brow tension, and under-eye structure
- Avoid fantasy distortion, supernatural haze, or obvious symbolic VFX

### PHOTO_STYLE
- High-resolution Korean psychological-thriller still
- Moderate depth of field with controlled spatial pressure
- Real photographic rendering only
- The frame should feel exact, premium, and psychologically uncomfortable without becoming abstract

### COLOR
- Cold gray-blue
- Washed beige
- Stale fluorescent white
- Faded institutional green undertones
- Low-saturation skin values that still remain fully human

### MOOD
- Repression
- Internal fracture
- Paranoia
- Instability under control
- Unease
- Severe mental pressure

### STRICT_RULES_APPEND
- Do not stylize the psychology into surrealism or horror fantasy
- Do not add impossible effects to communicate mental instability
- Let performance, composition, material realism, and light carry the psychological weight

### OUTPUT_TARGET_APPEND
- Ultra-realistic Korean psychological-thriller still defined by internal pressure
- Same recognizable uploaded user preserved under severe mental tension
- Premium live-action realism with psychological unease and exacting restraint
