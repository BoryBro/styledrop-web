# Romance

## Base — Romantic Chemistry Core

### INTRO
Transform the uploaded photo into a Korean romance film still built around the first unmistakable pull of emotional chemistry. The result must feel like a real high-resolution cinematic photograph — warm, intimate, cinematic, and authentic, with polished realism rather than fantasy softness.

### POSE_EXPRESSION
- Eye-level medium shot
- Relaxed shoulders and naturally open posture
- Eyes open and emotionally engaged
- Expression should feel soft, receptive, and quietly drawn toward someone or something just beyond the moment
- Body language should feel unguarded, gentle, and naturally magnetic rather than performatively romantic

### SCENE
- Everyday but elevated Korean romance environment such as a cafe window seat, bookstore aisle, city crosswalk, school walkway, neighborhood street, or softly lit public place
- The setting should feel believable, emotionally available, and visually clean without becoming luxury-commercial styled
- Background detail should support the feeling of possibility and connection without pulling focus
- The world should feel ordinary enough to become special through emotional attention

### LIGHTING
- Soft natural daylight or lightly warmed practical illumination
- Face should remain fully readable and naturally flattering
- Highlights may bloom gently to support romance, but skin must remain real and unretouched
- Lighting should feel tender, warm, and physically believable rather than cosmetic

### FILTER_DREAM_EFFECT
- Use refined romance-film softness instead of heavy dreamy wash
- Add subtle highlight diffusion, mild ambient glow, and fine grain
- Preserve natural pores, lip texture, and identity fidelity beneath the romantic treatment
- Avoid beauty-filter plasticity, app-like haze, wedding-photography gloss, or fantasy glow

### PHOTO_STYLE
- High-resolution Korean romance feature-film still
- Moderate depth of field with elegant emotional separation from the environment
- Real photographic rendering only
- The image should feel premium, sincere, and emotionally inviting rather than commercially polished

### COLOR
- Warm cream
- Pale peach
- Soft daylight blue
- Gentle gold
- Muted romantic neutrals with natural skin warmth

### MOOD
- Attraction
- Warmth
- Possibility
- Soft chemistry
- Openness
- Emotional tenderness

### STRICT_RULES_APPEND
- Do not turn the frame into wedding photography or beauty advertising
- Do not over-soften or glamorize the subject beyond realism
- Keep the romance cinematic, intimate, and human rather than cosmetic

### OUTPUT_TARGET_APPEND
- Ultra-realistic Korean romance still centered on emotional chemistry
- Same recognizable uploaded user preserved in a warm, believable romantic frame
- Elegant live-action tenderness and premium realism should define the final result
